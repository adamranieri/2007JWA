The Basics

Magic is a card game where two people face each other with decks of 60 cards. Each player has 20 life and when that hits 0, that player loses. To reduce each other's life total, each player has creatures that can attack players and block other creatures. Finally every turn a player may play 1 land. Land can be tapped in order to pay the cost of cards.

The Five Colors

There are 5 colors in this game, White, Blue, Black, Red, and Green. Each color can be paid for with its type of lands. Each does their own things in game but each is based on something. White is about law, order, and community. Blue is about intellect and gathering knowledge, red is about emotion. Black is about self-interest. Green is about nature.
